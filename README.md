# Marketing Analytics

This repository contains my marketing analytics projects showcasing data-driven insights, predictive modeling, and business intelligence dashboards. 
The projects cover channel performance, marketing mix modeling (MMM), customer lifetime value (LTV), and churn prediction. 

## Projects

1. [01_Channel_Performance](./01_Channel_Performance)  
   Analyze marketing channels to measure ROI, conversions, and campaign effectiveness. Includes dashboards and KPI tracking.

2. [02_MMM](./02_MMM)  
   Marketing Mix Modeling to quantify the impact of different channels on sales and recommend optimal budget allocation.

3. [03_LTV_Modeling](./03_LTV_Modeling)  
   Predict Customer Lifetime Value to identify high-value customers and inform acquisition and retention strategies.

4. [04_Churn_Prediction](./04_Churn_Prediction)  
   Predict which customers are likely to churn and suggest strategies to improve retention.
